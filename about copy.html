About us:

Who are we ?

Trimpack is a monthly car gear service for those who want to accessorize their cars internally or externally.

How it works ?

Customers who subscribe to any of our subscription models will be treated to mystery box of car products from emerging companies producing car seat covers,window tints,hardware and more for the automoblie market.

Every month Trimpack handpicks 8-10 items to be included in the box, which is kept a secret until the box is delivered.
